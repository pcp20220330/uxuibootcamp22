UX/UI Design

Project Overview

The skilled help neighbors can provide one another is often relegated to flyers posted on a community board or through word of mouth. Current apps have become overrun by professional service ads, scammers and polarized social feeds, dividing neighbors rather than connecting them. How might we encourage willingness among neighbors to reach out to each other for help?

My role

As a team, we performed user research and competitive analysis, and created user flow, wireframes and prototype in a 3-week sprint. I worked on this project as part of my University of Minnesota UX/UI Design course.
